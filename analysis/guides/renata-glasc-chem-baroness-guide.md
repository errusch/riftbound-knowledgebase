## Summary
Renata Glasc is an Order-Mind legend from Spiritforged designed around tag-team play, but has struggled to make her own synergies work in the meta. After four regional tournaments, she defaulted to building around strong standalone Mind/Order cards rather than her signature Gold Gear Token and ally-buff mechanics. Her ability grants +1 energy after reaching 5 points, enabling explosive late-game turns with expensive cards like SFD-202, OGN-105, and OGN-122. The token win condition (SFD-168 + SFD-084) has been relegated to the side deck.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Order + Mind
- Playstyle: Proactive reach-5-points-fast then gain +1 energy for big late turns
- Meta result: Failed to push own synergies; defaults to best-in-domain cards
- Signature spell SFD-202: takes control of opposing large unit (force 2 enemy units to fight each other); requires Gold Gear Tokens to absorb power cost
- Champion units SFD-171 (4-cost) and SFD-088 (5-cost) both underperforming
- Token win condition (SFD-168 + SFD-084) moved to side deck
- Late-game tools enabled by ability: SFD-202, OGN-105, OGN-122
- Strategy: Two builds (anti-Draven control first; then adapt for other rounds)
