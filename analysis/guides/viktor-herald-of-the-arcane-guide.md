## Summary
Viktor is a Mind-Order legend from Origins who generates tokens with his ability and is the premier token legend. In Spiritforged, he shifted to a control-heavy playstyle -- using removal to empty battlefields and then conquering with free tokens rather than buffing a large token army. OGN-117 (token generator in base) and SFD-168 (passive token generation) are core. Only OGN-117 champion card sees play; OGN-246 and OGN-266 are absent from competitive builds. Viktor consistently makes top cuts at regional events.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Mind + Order
- Playstyle: Removal-heavy control; tokens conquer empty battlefields
- Meta position: Consistently makes regional top cuts in Spiritforged
- Shifted from token aggro to control in response to Draven meta
- Only active champion: OGN-117 (token generator, stays safe in base); OGN-246 and OGN-266 not used
- Three card types needed: Removal, card draw (OGN-087/099/095/083), free token generation (OGN-117, SFD-168)
- Best-of-3 advantage: Control style harder to hate out with sidedecks vs token aggro
