## Summary
Volibear is a Fury-Body legend from Origins and the premier ramp deck, focused on playing 5+ might units to trigger his ability and accelerate rune generation. The ideal turn sequence is playing a 5-might unit on turn 2 (OGN-134 for going first, 3 runes when going second) to have 8 runes on turn 3. Cheap utility spells like OGN-043 and OGN-168 counter his big units by sending them back to base. OGN-160 (the Gear) is the ultimate payoff once in the late game, and OGN-161 is his ideal large unit (high might, Deflect, immediate impact).

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Body
- Playstyle: Ramp -- play 5+ might units to gain energy, then dominate with 10-12 cost threats
- Turn 2 5-might unit → 8 runes on turn 3 (ideal line)
- Ramp enablers: OGN-134 (going first), SFD-014 (5-might ramp trigger)
- Champion units: OGN-041 (10-cost, chosen champion), OGN-158 (12-cost) -- both too slow without reaction
- Signature OGN-250: board clear + conquest for 6 energy + 2 runes; high risk, use vs tapped opponent
- Ideal unit: OGN-161 (massive might, Deflect, immediate impact)
- Late-game: OGN-160 (Gear), OGN-038, OGN-041
- Counter-play: OGN-043, OGN-168 (bounce big units without removing them)
- Curve units: OGN-149 (5-cost removal), SFD-014
