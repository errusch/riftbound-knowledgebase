## Summary
Lucian is a Fury-Body legend from Spiritforged who builds around equipment cards and a conquering playstyle, making the top cut of every regional tournament. His ability triggers when conquering with equipped units, requiring the deck to include both equipment cards and a conqueror strategy -- with no room to deviate. SFD-113 (Weaponmaster) is his chosen champion and works well when going first paired with a 1-cost equipment. Signature spell SFD-184 (Relentless Pursuit) enables scoring, unit protection, and can be used reactively during opponent's turn.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Body
- Playstyle: Equipment-based conqueror; made top cut every regional tournament
- Must build: equipment cards + conqueror strategy (ability requires both)
- Chosen champion: SFD-113 (Weaponmaster; returns to base after conquering, forces spell removal)
- SFD-028 not seeing competitive play
- Signature: SFD-184 (Relentless Pursuit) -- conquer, protect unit, score during opponent's turn
- Key units: OGN-039 (draw), SFD-113 (scoring)
- Top curve: SFD-021 (resilient) or OGN-026 (counter reactive decks)
- Equipment buffs: might increases, Deflect granting, scoring/draw improvement
