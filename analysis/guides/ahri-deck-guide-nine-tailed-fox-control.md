## Summary
Control deck guide for Ahri (Nine-Tailed Fox), a Calm-Mind legend. Ahri uses spells to control the board and protect champion units like Sona-Harmonious and Ahri-Inquisitive. Wind Wall and Defy protect against hard removal. Strong into aggro, weaker into Viktor.

## Key Facts
Ahri = Calm + Mind domains; control/defensive playstyle; key units: Sona-Harmonious (hold runes open), Ahri-Inquisitive (reduce opponent might), Kai'Sa-Evolutionary, Viktor-Innovator; protection spells: Wind Wall, Defy; battlefields: Fortified Position, Targon's Peak, Reaver's Row; finished 2nd in first TTS tournament; good into aggro, weak into Viktor; control takes time to optimize
