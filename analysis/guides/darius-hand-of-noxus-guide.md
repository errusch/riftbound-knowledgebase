## Summary
Darius combines Fury and Order domains as an aggressive midrange legend that gains an extra energy when he plays a card first each turn. This forces him to play two cards per turn (Legion keyword synergy), making him explosive in the early game but vulnerable to running out of cards against control decks. The Spiritforged meta sees Darius building around OGN-242 for special summoning, with units ranging 2-6 might buffed by SFD-166 or SFD-161. Tournament results suggest this is not a winning strategy for Darius but likely his best option.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Order
- Playstyle: Rush/midrange, requires playing 2 cards per turn to use ability
- Key card: OGN-242 (core synergy for special summons)
- Special summon support: OGN-226, SFD-165
- Buffs: SFD-166, SFD-161
- Signature: OGN-027 (grows to 7 might, moves twice/turn); OGN-254 and OGN-243 rarely played
- OGN-027 competes with SFD-021 in Spiritforged
- Weakness: hand exhaustion vs control; ability less impactful as game progresses
