## Summary
Spiritforged Body domain card evaluation. Body was top domain in Origins (OGS-019, OGN-269) but Spiritforged new cards are more situational. Key new cards: Punch First (SFD-097, 5-might for 1 energy late), Strike Down, Warmog Armor, Here to Help, Lucian-Merciless, Ruin Runner, Yone-Blademaster, Akshan.
