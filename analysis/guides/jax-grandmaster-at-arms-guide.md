## Summary
Jax is a Calm-Body equipment specialist who struggled throughout Spiritforged due to gear removal being popular in side decks and the Chaos-heavy meta. His ability transfers equipment between units for free, requiring two units plus an equipped card and proper turn sequencing. In a metagame where units get bounced frequently, maintaining this setup is difficult. Top performing builds focus on resilient units protected by Calm spells (OGN-045, SFD-045) and the buff synergy to draw via OGN-282. Card draw remains a significant weakness.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Body
- Playstyle: Equipment-heavy midrange, unit protection
- Meta challenge: Gear removal popular in side decks; Chaos bounce disrupts setup
- Ability requires: 2 units + 1 equipped card, specific sequencing
- Chosen champion: SFD-194; signature spell is a staple
- SFD-054 vulnerable to non-targeting removal (bounce, mind reduction)
- Card draw weakness: relies on OGN-058, SFD-194
- All three featured battlefields have draw effects
- Unit protection: OGN-045, SFD-045
- Buff synergy used for OGN-282 draw trigger
