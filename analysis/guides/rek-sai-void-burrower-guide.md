## Summary
Rek'Sai is a Fury-Order legend still searching for the right balance in Spiritforged. Her ability reveals and potentially plays cards when conquering, requiring units to survive from turn to turn -- difficult given her domains lack strong unit protection tools. Signature spell SFD-188 (fetches top two cards of deck, play one draw one for 1 rune recycle) is the standout card and provides key consistency. The deck builds as aggressive midrange using Fury's conquest tools paired with Order's strong removal duo (OGN-213 + OGN-209).

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Order
- Playstyle: Aggressive midrange conqueror; ability triggers when unit survives to next turn
- Weakness: Lacks unit protection tools for surviving between turns
- Standout signature: SFD-188 (see top 2, play 1 draw 1 for 1 rune recycle)
- Chosen champion: SFD-029 (1 copy); SFD-170 not used
- Removal tandem: OGN-213 + OGN-209 (best in Riftbound per guide)
- Energy tools: SFD-175 (differentiator vs other Fury legends)
- Key units: OGN-012 + OGN-027 (great duo for multi-card-per-turn legends), OGN-037 (recurring value)
- Support cards: 2-cost buffs/removals playable alongside SFD-188
