## Summary
Rumble is a Fury-Mind legend from Spiritforged built around the Mech tribe synergy. Sharing domains with Kai'Sa (considered one of the best legends), being forced into a tribe-only strategy is a significant limitation. The best scenario is going first and conquering on turn two with a mech to establish a 3-might defender. Both champion units (SFD-026 and SFD-089) see play and synergize with each other. The deck supplements the mech package with strong Fury/Mind standalone cards (OGN-039, OGN-116, OGN-029) that transcend the tribe restriction.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Mind (same as Kai'Sa)
- Playstyle: Tribe-focused (Mechs) midrange; limited by needing to reveal strategy upfront
- Best scenario: Go first → conquer turn 2 with mech → 3-might defender
- Champion units: SFD-026 (mech summoner, turn 2 play) + SFD-089 (payoff, improves mech resiliency) -- 3-3 split
- Signature spell SFD-182: situational (dodge removal for SFD-026/SFD-089, or support final showdowns); 1 copy enough
- Cheap mech summon: SFD-076 (always 2-cost in this deck)
- Non-mech staples: OGN-039, OGN-116, OGN-029
- Main limitation: Simple gameplan easily played around by informed opponents
