## Summary
Lux is a Mind-Order starter legend who plays a tap-out control style, casting big spells to trigger her passive ability and slowly out-value opponents. Spiritforged strengthened her with more removal (SFD-164) and a new win condition (SFD-168). The early game involves managing the board with removal while creating Gold Gear Tokens; the mid-game goal is safely developing SFD-168. Chosen champion is now OGS-014 (2 extra runes per spell cost when alive) over OGS-006 in most Spiritforged builds.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Mind + Order (starter legend)
- Playstyle: Control, tap-out big spells, value accumulation
- Spiritforged additions: SFD-164 (removal), SFD-168 (win condition)
- Chosen champion: OGS-014 (2 extra runes per spell cost); OGS-006 (grows 8 might each turn with OGS-021)
- OGS-022 (signature spell) pushed out of use by Spiritforged additions
- Strategy: Early removal + Gold Gear Tokens → SFD-168 safely developed → token conquest
- Key card: SFD-084 (uses tokens as fodder)
- Win requires navigating early/mid game without allowing snowball
