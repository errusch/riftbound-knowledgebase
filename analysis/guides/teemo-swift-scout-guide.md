## Summary
Teemo is a Mind-Chaos legend built around the Hidden mechanic, using his ability to make Hidden an energy cost rather than a power cost, and recycling his champion unit OGN-197 by picking it up from the board. However, in Spiritforged, the Hidden mechanic struggles because all top legends pack spells to remove units from battlefields. As a result, Teemo has become a "bootleg Ezreal" copying his deck, which led to his best regional result but doesn't tap into his unique strengths. OGN-264 and OGN-121 are not seeing competitive play.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Mind + Chaos
- Playstyle: Hidden mechanic bluffing and ambush; Spiritforged forces pivot to Ezreal-style build
- Legend ability: Hidden costs energy (not power); can return OGN-197 to hand from board
- Champion: OGN-197 (chosen champion); OGN-264 and OGN-121 not competitive
- Hidden mechanic struggles: Top legends all pack battlefield removal spells
- Best result: Copying Ezreal's deck (OGN-116, gear synergy for OGN-091 readying)
- Prognosis: Won't improve significantly without Hidden getting stronger tools
