## Summary
Jinx is a Fury-Chaos legend built around the discard mechanic. Her ability draws two cards when she starts a turn with one or zero cards in hand, making holding back on discards unnecessary. OGN-202 (5-cost) has become more popular than OGN-030 in Spiritforged due to competition from strong 3-cost cards like SFD-149. The deck uses energy-cheating mechanics through OGN-194 (summoned via OGN-291/OGN-183/SFD-122) and cost-manipulation via OGN-012, SFD-012, and OGN-195.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Chaos
- Playstyle: Discard-driven explosive aggro; draws 2 when hand empty at turn start
- Champion options: OGN-030 (2-cost, Accelerate+Assault 2) vs OGN-202 (5-cost, grows+multi-move)
- OGN-202 more popular in Spiritforged (SFD-149 competes with OGN-030)
- Key early unit: OGN-185 (discard+draw each move)
- Energy cheating: OGN-194 (via OGN-291/OGN-183/SFD-122)
- Cost manipulation: OGN-012, SFD-012, OGN-195
- Removal tools adapted to meta: SFD-145, SFD-136, OGN-172, OGN-168
- OGN-252 provides discard on demand + removal flexibility
