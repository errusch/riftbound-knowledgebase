## Summary
Leona is an Order-Calm legend that combines the Stun mechanic with buffs to her own units, making her a showdown specialist. Her ability rewards stunning opposing units by buffing friendly ones. The goal is to force opponents into using expensive spell removal (since their units get stunned in showdowns), then protect those units with counter-spells to create a massive resource advantage. OGN-238 (4-cost champion) is a solid standalone and appears in other legends' side decks. Her weakness is interacting with units at the opponent's base (OGN-262 and OGN-213 only work at battlefields).

## Key Facts
- Domains: Order + Calm
- Playstyle: Stun-buff combo, showdown dominance
- Key champion: OGN-238 (4 might; grows to 5 on defense or stuns on attack; used in other legends' sidedecks)
- OGN-079 (6-cost champion): powerful but needs stun support; chosen champion slot
- Signature spell OGN-262: expensive (2 rune recycle) but flips combat tempo; late-game tool
- Core duo: OGN-232 + OGN-238
- Hold tool: OGN-073 (once battlefield secured)
- Support spells: OGN-058 (buff+draw), SFD-040, SFD-045, OGN-207
- Weakness: Can't reach units in opponent's base; OGN-067 added to prevent multi-unit prep
