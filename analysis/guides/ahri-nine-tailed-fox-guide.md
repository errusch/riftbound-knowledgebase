## Summary
Ahri is a Calm-Mind legend built around holding battlefields rather than attacking. She manipulates unit might to control combat, leveraging Blue and Green cards that punish single attackers (SFD-040, SFD-053) while packing many buffs for friendly units. Her game plan is to seize a battlefield early and then hold it, forcing opponents to overcommit to specific locations. The pace has become faster in Spiritforged, requiring more flexibility and interaction.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Mind
- Playstyle: Battlefield control, hold-oriented
- Champion units: OGN-066 (Blue, game 1) and OGN-119 (Green, side deck)
- OGN-256 (signature spell) difficult in Chaos-heavy meta; mainly used as hidden card
- Key hold cards: OGN-067, OGN-073, SFD-040, OGN-045, OGN-057
- Key conquer cards: SFD-034, OGN-058, OGN-116 (three copies standard)
- Hardest part: getting set on a battlefield early
