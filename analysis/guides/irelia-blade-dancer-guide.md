## Summary
Irelia is a Chaos-Calm legend from Spiritforged and Draven's biggest competition, ranking second in most meta metrics (popularity, conversion rate, tournament wins). Her ability recycles runes to move friendly units, making SFD-057 (signature champion with Deflect and growth potential) extremely threatening. The deck is focused on protecting key units and moving them around battlefields using Chaos and Calm support spells. A focused, one-trick-pony strategy that excels when the game plan comes together but has yet to win a major regional final.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Chaos + Calm
- Playstyle: Aggressive movement, unit protection, timing-based ability use
- Meta position: #2 legend by most metrics in Spiritforged; failed to reach regional finals
- Key champion: SFD-057 (Deflect keyword, grows in might)
- SFD-048 (stellar sidekick, enables card draw)
- SFD-148 replacing extra champion copies in top decks
- Buff support: SFD-196, SFD-045, OGN-045, SFD-051 (unit protection)
- Movement spells: OGN-184, OGN-168, OGN-173
- Turn 1 plays: OGN-176, SFD-036
- SFD-141 not seeing play; SFD-195 ability discourages early power-cost cards
