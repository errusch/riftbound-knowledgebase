## Summary
Master Yi tempo/defensive deck guide. Body-Calm legend that holds both battlefields as long as possible. 22 units, 15 spells, 3 gear. Skill-dependent reactive gameplay. Champions: Master Yi-Honed (Ganking, 6-might 7-cost) and Qiyana-Victorious.

## Key Facts
Master Yi = Body + Calm domains; 22 units/15 spells/3 gear; hold-both-battlefields strategy; Discipline = buff spell; Defy = counter enemy moves; Pit Rookie and Clockwork Keeper = early value; Whiteflame Protector = game closer +8 buff; Master Yi-Honed = 6-might 7-cost with Ganking, enters ready; Qiyana-Victorious = 4-cost 4-might Deflect draws on conquer; weak against Viktor (board hate); based on Presto's 2nd place list Aug 9 2025
