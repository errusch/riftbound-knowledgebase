## Summary
Lee Sin is a Calm-Body legend built around the Buff mechanic, using a midrange unit strategy supported by Calm spells that protect key units. His play pattern is to get one large unit stuck on the board and stack buffs while keeping opponents from removing it with spells. OGN-151 has become the chosen champion (effectively a 6/8 with one buffed unit alongside), while OGN-258 serves as a late-game combo punish against players who develop two large units. Card draw is his main weakness, addressed by running 3x SFD-048.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Body
- Playstyle: Midrange buff-stacking, unit protection
- Chosen champion: OGN-151 (effectively 6/8 with one buffed ally; Accelerate option)
- OGN-078 (champion) sees no competitive play in Spiritforged
- Signature spell: OGN-258 (1 copy staple -- punishes opponent's two large units; synergy with OGN-058)
- Key synergy: OGN-058 can equalize two units' might to trigger OGN-258 mutual destruction
- Unit threats: SFD-113, SFD-048, SFD-047, OGN-164
- Card draw weakness: 3x SFD-048 + OGN-282 battlefield
- Protection: OGN-045, SFD-097 (edge showdowns)
