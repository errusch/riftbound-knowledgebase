## Summary
Guide to movement mechanics in Riftbound, focusing on Ganking (moving between battlefields) and advanced movement combos. Ganking allows units to move from battlefield to battlefield, enabling surprise wins.

## Key Facts
Exhausted units cannot move independently; cannot move between battlefields without Ganking keyword or card effects; Ganking = "this unit can move from battlefield to battlefield"; Yasuo decks specialize in Ganking; moving to enemy battlefield triggers Showdown; Unforgiven card enables unexhausted movement; Kayn Unleashed powerful with movement
