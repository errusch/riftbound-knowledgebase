## Summary
Overview of all six Riftbound domains: Fury, Calm, Mind, Body, Chaos, and Order. Each domain has a distinct identity and play style. Decks are limited to two domains based on their chosen Legend.

## Key Facts
Six domains: Fury (aggression/damage/discard), Calm (defense/hold/tricks/movement), Mind (card draw/hidden/planning/gear), Body (high-might units/buffs), Chaos (movement/return from trash/bounce), Order (removal/tokens/structure); each Legend has exactly 2 domains; Accelerate and Assault keywords = Fury; Hidden keyword = Mind; Ganking movement = Calm; Recycling = Mind
