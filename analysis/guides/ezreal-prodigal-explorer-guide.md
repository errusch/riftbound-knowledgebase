## Summary
Ezreal is a Chaos-Mind legend whose ability draws a card when he targets two enemy cards per turn. His complexity requires a finely tuned deck balancing targeting effects with proactive pressure. After a slow start in Spiritforged, Ezreal reached Top 32 in Fuzhou and Top 4 in Chengdu regionals. Key cards with the Repeat keyword or multi-target abilities let him reliably trigger his draw. Gold Gear Tokens (SFD-T03) are crucial to avoid falling behind on runes due to the many power-cost cards in the deck.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Chaos + Mind
- Playstyle: Card advantage through targeting enemy units/gears twice per turn
- Tournament results: Top 32 Fuzhou, Top 4 Chengdu (on the rise in Spiritforged)
- Must trigger ability: target 2 enemy cards per turn using repeat/multi-target spells
- Gold Gear Token generation: SFD-069, SFD-130, SFD-134, OGN-204
- Signature cards: OGN-195 (3-cost staple in Chaos), SFD-200 (replay unit + 3 damage)
- SFD-082 sees no competitive play
- Key spell: SFD-080 (synergizes with ability + removes SFD-130)
