## Summary
Draven is a Chaos-Fury legend widely regarded as the best competitive pick in the Spiritforged metagame. His ability draws a card whenever he wins any combat (as attacker or defender), creating constant decision dilemmas for opponents about whether to fight. The deck has two main builds: standard midrange (won Fuzhou and Chengdu regionals in China) and "Miracle Draven" (won a large Italian tournament on January 10th). Key battlefield OGN-289 lets him run support cards without sacrificing midrange unit development.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Chaos + Fury
- Playstyle: Explosive midrange; draws on any combat win
- Tournament wins: Fuzhou Regional, Chengdu Regional (China); Italian large tournament (Jan 10)
- Two builds: Standard Midrange and Miracle Draven
- Chosen champion: SFD-020 (showdown-oriented midrange)
- Key cards: SFD-130 (energy efficiency), OGN-183, SFD-122 (deck dig), OGN-172 (bounce large threats), OGN-192 (late-game, max 2 copies)
- Key battlefield: OGN-289
- Signature: SFD-020 permanent +3 might as reaction; SFD-148 in some sidedecks
