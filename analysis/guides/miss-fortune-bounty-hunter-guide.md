## Summary
Miss Fortune is a Body-Chaos legend built around the Ganking mechanic (units moving between battlefields). Her ability grants any unit Ganking, which is especially powerful on massive units opponents cannot easily remove. She is a ramp-oriented strategy using OGN-160 as the core payoff, specializing against other late-game strategies. Signature card OGN-268 is a low-cost board wipe against tokens; OGN-162 (chosen champion) enables scoring twice in one turn via the specific Accelerate combo. OGN-193 counters ganking risk by challenging empty battlefields.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Body + Chaos
- Playstyle: Ramp → OGN-160 → Gank massive units to score final 2 points simultaneously
- Ability: Grant any unit Ganking (key for scoring 8th point requiring 2-battlefield conquest)
- Chosen champion: OGN-162 (readies units after moving; Accelerate for double-attack combo)
- OGN-268 (signature spell): board wipe vs tokens; scales with runes recycled
- OGN-193: attack empty battlefields after clearing with Chaos bounce/removal
- Core late-game: OGN-160 (Gear), OGN-192 (hand disruption), SFD-150 (anti-control)
- OGN-169 used for early game tempo control
