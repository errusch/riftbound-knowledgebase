## Summary
Kai'Sa is a Fury-Mind legend whose ability lets her cast spells without recycling runes (ignoring power costs), allowing her to play powerful removal spells like OGN-029 and OGN-009 without sacrificing unit development. In Spiritforged she has shifted to a control-heavy playstyle aimed at countering Draven. OGN-248 lost appeal after an errata changed reflexive triggers. Gold Gear Tokens further reduce power costs, letting her win long games with OGN-105, OGN-114, OGN-116, and OGN-122 as closers.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Mind
- Playstyle: Spell-control, power-cost immunity, counter-Draven strategy
- Won 3rd Regional tournament in China (pre-OGN-248 errata)
- Ability: Cast spells without recycling runes (ignores power costs)
- OGN-248 (signature) nerfed -- must announce all damage at once now; OGN-105 replaced it
- OGN-039 appears but only 1 copy (chosen champion) unlike other Fury legends
- OGN-112 sees zero competitive play
- Gold Gear Tokens (SFD-T03) further alleviate power costs
- Win condition: OGN-122 (when opponent out of resources)
- Disposable conquerors: OGN-096, OGN-087
