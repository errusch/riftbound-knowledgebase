## Summary
Sett is a Body-Order legend from Origins built around the Buff mechanic and showdown dominance. His ability recalls a defeated unit instead of letting it die -- effectively generating massive card advantage when used to save a unit from removal (becoming "draw 2"). The deck stacks buffs on resilient units that are difficult to remove, then uses free/cheap support spells to play even when all runes are exhausted. While no longer a top-tier competitor in Spiritforged, OGN-270 remains a must-own staple for the legend.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Body + Order
- Playstyle: Buff-stacking midrange; ability recalls defeated units (save from removal = draw 2)
- Meta: Not top-tier in Spiritforged but still competitively playable
- Champion: OGN-164 (1 copy chosen champion); OGN-240 cut for SFD-113 and OGN-232
- Staple: OGN-270 (buff + contest battlefield; must-own for Sett)
- Buff cards: SFD-108, OGN-232 (gains from buffs), SFD-098 (self-buffs)
- Free spell condition: "Spend a buff as additional cost to ignore this spell's cost" (e.g., OGN-207)
- Draw engine: OGN-282 battlefield (pay buff cost to draw)
- Non-removable unit: SFD-105; conqueror: SFD-113
