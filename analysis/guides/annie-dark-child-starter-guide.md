## Summary
Annie operates in Fury and Chaos domains, focusing on initiative and rune efficiency rather than a specific synergy. She excels as an early-game aggressor, using cheap removal (red cards) and utility spells (purple cards) to control battlefields and force opponents into uncomfortable positions. Her key strength is playing cards both on her own turn and during the opponent's turn using the two runes she readies at end of turn. Cards like OGN-039, OGS-010, and OGN-201 help prevent running out of cards.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Fury + Chaos
- Playstyle: Aggressive early snowball with initiative control
- Key cards: OGN-039, OGN-185, OGN-172, SFD-145, OGN-176 (score when going second), OGN-004 (showdown support)
- OGS-010 is main signature card; OGS-001 and OGS-017 also available
- Pairs two runes at end of turn for reactive spell use
