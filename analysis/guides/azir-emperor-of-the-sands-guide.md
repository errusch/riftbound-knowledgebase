## Summary
Azir is a Calm-Order legend that plays around equipment cards to trigger his ability and create Sand Soldiers as tokens. Unlike other token legends, he uses minimal raw removal (only OGN-209 and OGN-213), instead focusing on a proactive gear-first strategy. The deck requires 9-12 equipment cards to reliably use his ability each turn. SFD-198 is a key payoff card rewarding the gear-heavy build.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Order
- Playstyle: Equipment-focused conqueror with token generation
- Requires 9-12 equipment cards to trigger ability consistently
- Key equipment: SFD-153 (highest might/cost), SFD-042 (creates tokens)
- Champion: SFD-177 (1 copy, Accelerate keyword for instant conquest); SFD-050 sees more play with Ornn
- Signature spell SFD-198 is a staple (rewards gear-heavy builds)
- Hidden card tools: SFD-154, OGN-213, SFD-043, SFD-163
- Minimal removal: OGN-209, OGN-213 only
