## Summary
Beginner's guide to constructing a Riftbound deck. Covers choosing a Legend, building the Main Deck (40 cards), Rune Pool (12 cards), Battlefields (3 cards), and Sideboard (0 or 8 cards).

## Key Facts
Main Deck: 40 cards total; Rune Pool: 12 cards; Battlefields: 3 cards; Sideboard: exactly 0 or 8 cards; Chosen Champion starts on board not in deck; limited to 2 domains from Legend; each Legend has unique ability; domains restrict available cards; deckbuilder available on riftbound.gg
