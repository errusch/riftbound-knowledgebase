## Summary
Foundational guide to battlefield mechanics, map control, and unit trading in Riftbound. Covers how to distribute units between two battlefields, when to trade units, and core gameplay principles.

## Key Facts
Two battlefields per game (home + away); must conquer to score points; unit distribution critical; trading sacrifices one unit to remove opponent's; trading while ahead vs behind; some decks use non-unit strategies (Kai'Sa); ganking allows cross-battlefield movement; 3 battlefields in deck, 1 chosen randomly
