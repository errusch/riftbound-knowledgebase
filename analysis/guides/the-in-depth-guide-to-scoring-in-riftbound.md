## Summary
Comprehensive guide to Riftbound's 8-point scoring system. Explains the lethal range (6 points), Conqueror vs Holder strategies, and how to close out games. Critical ruling: to win via conquering, you must have scored all other battlefields first that turn.

## Key Facts
Win at 8 points (11 in 2v2); last point only via conquer if all other BFs scored same turn; lethal range = 6 points; Conqueror deck targets 2-4-6 progression then double conquer; Holder deck wins at 7 via holding; OGN-116 key closing card; OGN-122 prevents opponent from untapping; Conqueror=even scoring Holder=odd scoring
