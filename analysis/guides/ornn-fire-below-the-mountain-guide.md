## Summary
Ornn is a Calm-Mind legend from Spiritforged who builds almost entirely around SFD-085 (a 6-cost unit with Deflect 2 that grows stronger with more gears in play). His ability eliminates power costs for gear cards, making OGN-081 free. He has three unique one-copy signature spells instead of one card in triplicate; SFD-192 (grants Ganking to SFD-085) is the best performer. The strategy is vulnerable to gear removal (OGN-224) and Chaos bounce, and has yet to achieve a major tournament win.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Mind
- Playstyle: Single-threat strategy around SFD-085 (Deflect 2, grows with gears)
- Three 1-copy signature spells: SFD-192 (Ganking grant, best), SFD-058 (not used), third not specified
- OGN-081 is free with Ornn's ability (no power cost)
- Core strategy: Protect SFD-085, grant it Ganking via SFD-192, control with Calm spells
- SFD-050 more popular alongside Ornn than Azir
- Counter: OGN-077 (force double removal), OGN-045/SFD-045 (spell protection)
- Gear suite: SFD-059 + equipment for growing SFD-085
- Movement: SFD-043 or SFD-192 for ganking
- Weakness: Gear removal (OGN-224), Chaos bounce; no tournament win yet
