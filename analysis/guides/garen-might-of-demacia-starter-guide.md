## Summary
Garen is a Might-Body legend designed as a starter for new players and is widely regarded as the weakest competitive legend in Riftbound. His token-based approach (signature cards OGS-024 and OGS-013) is affordable and beginner-friendly but struggles in tournament environments. Competitive Garen players bypass signature cards entirely and build around OGN-160 and other strong late-game Order/Body cards. Spiritforged added SFD-179 to support his ability but did not meaningfully improve his competitive standing.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Might + Body (starter legend)
- Competitive tier: Weakest legend in the game per community consensus
- Signature cards: OGS-024, OGS-013 (token synergy, beginner-friendly), OGS-007 (weak)
- Competitive approach: Skip signature cards, build around OGN-160 and strong late-game cards
- Spiritforged addition: SFD-179 (triggers legend ability)
- Designed as entry-point legend per Riot's stated goals
