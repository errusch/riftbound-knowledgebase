## Summary
Sivir is a Body-Chaos legend from Spiritforged who leverages power-costs to recycle runes while creating Gold Gear Tokens to offset future costs -- a high-risk, high-reward playstyle. Her chosen champion SFD-143 can be played turn two and scales with rune recycling. Two competitive builds exist: an OGN-160 ramp variant (similar to Miss Fortune) that consistently makes Day 2 but hasn't won a tournament, and a Chaos "miracle" build (SFD-149-focused) that achieved one great result but hasn't gained wider adoption.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Body + Chaos
- Playstyle: Power-cost exploitation to create Gold Gear Tokens; high-risk strategy
- Meta: Two builds -- OGN-160 ramp (consistent Day 2) and Chaos miracle (one great result)
- Chosen champion: SFD-143 (turn 2 playable; scales with rune recycling, gains Ganking)
- SFD-120 (other champion): high power cost; competes with OGN-192, SFD-105
- Signature SFD-204 (Rallying Cry): Underused -- deck leans Chaos not Body; readying units less impactful
- Chaos build core: SFD-149 + Chaos synergy cards
- Both builds agree on key support cards
- OGN-160 build specializes vs other late-game strategies like Miss Fortune
