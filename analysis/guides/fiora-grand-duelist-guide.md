## Summary
Fiora is a Calm-Order legend that gains extra runes when her units reach 5 might, which she uses to offset power-cost cards. She focuses on resilient units that can dominate showdowns, becoming Mighty with buffs. Fiora reached the finals of both Fuzhou and Chengdu regionals in China -- one build was midrange with equipment, the other used OGN-242 as a core component. Her signature spell SFD-206 (Riposte) is a game-changer in late stages, forcing opponents to respect held runes even without having it in hand.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Order
- Playstyle: Midrange buff-oriented, power-cost mitigation via 5-might threshold
- Tournament: Finalist at Fuzhou AND Chengdu regionals
- Champion units: OGN-232 (3 copies, strong standalone), SFD-180 (chosen champion slot)
- Signature spell SFD-206 (Riposte): recycles 2 runes, counters opponent spell with buff
- Key buff cards: OGN-136, SFD-097, OGN-164
- Removal: OGN-213, OGN-128
- Second build uses OGN-242 + OGN-226 + OGN-234 synergy
