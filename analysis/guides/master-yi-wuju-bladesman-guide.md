## Summary
Master Yi is a Body-Calm legend from the Origins starter set who was a top competitor during Origins but declined in Spiritforged. His ability gives his solo defender +2 might, incentivizing single-unit battlefield defense and preserving runes for Calm support spells. He aims to hold battlefields with one unit and dominate late with high-cost Body/Calm cards. New Spiritforged additions SFD-036 (early play) and SFD-105 (late-game unit) improve reliability. SFD-115 synergizes uniquely with his ability (2-cost becomes effectively 6 might with the +2 bonus).

## Key Facts
- Domains: Body + Calm (starter legend)
- Playstyle: Hold-oriented, single defender, late-game dominant
- Meta: Top performer in Origins; solid but not championship material in Spiritforged
- Ability: Solo defender gets +2 might (discourages multi-unit defense at one location)
- Chosen champion: OGS-009 (strong late-game unit ready to deploy)
- OGS-020 and OGS-004 not competitive vs OGN-077/OGN-082/OGN-161
- New Spiritforged additions: SFD-036 (turn 1 play), SFD-105 (late game), SFD-115 (2-cost → 6 might effective)
- OGN-043: forces opposing base units into showdown (cheap removal alternative)
