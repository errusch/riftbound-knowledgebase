## Summary
Kai'Sa Tempo deck guide. Traditional tempo deck with cheap threats and removal. 19 Units, 19 Spells, 2 Gear. Aims to deploy early threats and use removal to stay ahead. Champions: Kai'Sa-Survivor and Darius-Trifarian.

## Key Facts
Kai'Sa = Fury + Mind; 19 units/19 spells/2 gear; mostly 4-cost or less units; Void Seeker = damage removal; Smoke Screen = combat trick; Thousand-Tailed Watcher = emergency threat; Kai'Sa-Survivor and Darius-Trifarian as mid-game champs; battlefields: Void Gate (+1 damage), Windswept Hillock, third neutral; weak against Sett (beefy units dodge cheap removal); based on Kuvira's 1st place list July 26 2025
