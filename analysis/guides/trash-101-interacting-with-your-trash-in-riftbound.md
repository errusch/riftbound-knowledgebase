## Summary
Guide to Riftbound's graveyard (trash) mechanics. Covers recycling (returning to deck - Mind domain), returning from trash to hand (Chaos domain), and playing from trash (Fury/Immortal Phoenix). Jinx is the champion built around trash interaction.

## Key Facts
Trash = graveyard; dead units and resolved spells go to trash; Recycling = put back in deck (Mind domain specialty); Returning = trash to hand (Chaos domain via Cemetery Attendant); Playing from trash = Fury domain (Immortal Phoenix); Jinx champion centers on discard/trash; Garbage Grabber recycles 3 while drawing; Guerilla Warfare is Chaos/other split
