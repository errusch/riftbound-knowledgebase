## Summary
Yasuo is a Calm-Chaos legend from Origins who combines two support-heavy domains (Calm for unit buffs/counters, Chaos for energy cheating and movement). His ability allows treating an exhausted unit as ready -- either sending it to a battlefield or returning it to base for safety -- but this requires heavy rune investment on top of already support-heavy gameplay. Yasuo struggles in Spiritforged, with Irelia being a more reliable Calm-Chaos option. His best result came from running OGN-205 as champion, attempting to snowball high-value units protected by SFD-051 and OGN-077.

## Key Facts
- Domains: Calm + Chaos
- Playstyle: Support-heavy tempo; ability reuses exhausted units (conquest or safety retreat)
- Meta: Consistently poor results in large tournaments; Irelia is strictly better in same domains
- Chosen champion: OGN-076 (6-cost, opportunistic vs tapped opponent); best result with OGN-205
- OGN-205 (signature): fragile, high-investment; better as side deck vs low-removal decks
- OGN-260 (signature spell): 2-rune recycle; usually cut for OGN-173
- Strategy: Snowball SFD-048 or OGN-205 protected by SFD-051/OGN-077 + support spells
- Article note: "Or play the same deck as Irelia with a different, worse legend"
