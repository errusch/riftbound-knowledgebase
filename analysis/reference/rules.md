# Riftbound Technical Manual

## Product Information
- **Release Date**: October 31st, 2025 (Origins set)
- **Developer/Publisher**: Riot Games in partnership with UVS Games.
- **Sets**:
    - *Origins*: Launch set (Oct 31, 2025). nearly 300 cards.
    - *Spiritforged*: Second set (Released Feb 2026). 220+ cards, 60+ alt-arts. Features the **"Spirit Blossom"** thematic for accessories.
- **Official Platform**: riftbound.carde.io (Event management)

## The Merch Economy (Buying Direct)
Starting with *Spiritforged*, Riot moved to a **Drawing-Based System** for direct merch store purchases to combat botting.
- **Entry Window**: 48 hours to register via Riot Account.
- **Selection**: Random selection of verified accounts.
- **Purchase Window**: 24-hour locked cart. If you are drawn for multiple items (Display, Rumble Deck, Fiora Deck), you must purchase all or none.
- **Limit**: Strictly 1 of any specific product per person.

## Key Dates & Meta Events
- **Feb 3, 2026**: Spiritforged Drawing Registration opens.
- **Feb 9, 2026**: Purchase links sent to drawing winners.
- **Feb 13, 2026**: Spiritforged Release & Spirit Blossom accessories launch.

## Pack & Deck Architecture
- **Booster Packs**: 14 cards total.
    - 7 Commons, 3 Uncommons, 2 Foil Rares or better, 1 Foil (any rarity), 1 Token slot.
- **Display Boxes**: 24 packs per display. 
    - Pull Rates: Avg 6+ Epics and 2+ Special Alt-Arts per box.
- **Preconstructed Champion Decks**: 56 cards.
    - *Origins*: Jinx (Aggro), Viktor (Swarm).
    - *Spiritforged*: Rumble (Mecha-Synergy/Scaling), Fiora (Precision/Buffing).
    - Includes 1 Legend (Champion) card.
    - Includes 3 Rares.
    - Includes 1 Booster Pack for immediate customization.
- **Bulk Rune SKU (RB-01RS01-EN)**: 324 Runes total (54 of each domain). Sold in a storage box to support limited play.

## Organized Play (OP) Hierarchy
- **Level 1: Nexus Nights**: Weekly, flexible format.
    - *Spiritforged Update*: Promo packs now include **Proving Grounds** cards (Legends, Champions, Signatures) for increased accessibility.
- **Level 2: Summoner Skirmish**: Monthly premier local events (Month 2 and 3). 1v1 Constructed, Best of 3. Winners earn a **Round 1 Bye** for Regional Qualifiers.
- **Level 3: Regional Qualifiers**: Open tournaments. Direct path to Regional Championships.
- **Level 4: Regional Championships**: Invitation-only (Qualification required).
- **Level 5: World Championship**: The pinnacle of Riftbound competition (Launching in 2027).

## Season Structure (3-Month Cycle)
- **Month 1: Set Launch & Pre-Rift**:
    - **Pre-Rift Format**: 15-card mini-precon (Legend + Champion Unit) + 5 Boosters + 1 Promo.
- **Month 2 & 3: Competitive Grind**:
    - Focus on Summoner Skirmishes and meta stabilization.

## Prize Policy
- **Official OP Events**: Cash prizing is prohibited. Stores are encouraged to use Store Credit or Booster Packs as additional prize support alongside official Promos and Playmats.
- **Learn-to-Play**: Introductory events for new players.
- **Nexus Night**: Standard weekly organized play. Stores initially received 3 kits (144 promo packs) to cover the period between *Origins* and *Spiritforged*.
- **Summoner Skirmish**: High-tier monthly competitive events (Dec, Jan, March, April etc.).
- **Pre-Rift**: Specialized pre-release events for upcoming sets.
- **Release Events**: Official launch celebrations.

## Player Requirements
- **Digital Infrastructure**: 
    - Players must have a **Riot Account** (with Game Name and Tagline assigned).
    - Players must have a **Carde.io Account**.
    - Accounts must be linked via `locator.riftbound.uvsgames.com/events` to participate in official events.
- **Participation Flow**: Identity verification is handled through the Riot ecosystem, ensuring professional tracking of rankings and tournament history.

## Tournament Management
- **Platform**: Admin interface at `https://riftbound.carde.io/admin/events`.
- **Registration**: Managed through specific windows; late registration is generally not permitted.
- **Event Statuses**: "Requirements Met," "Registered," and "Paid" are mandatory statuses for tournament pairing.
