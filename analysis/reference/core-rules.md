# Riftbound Technical Manual: Core Rules v1.2 (Verified)

## Fundamental Laws
- **Golden Rule**: Card text supersedes rules.
- **Silver Rule**: Interpretation follows the rules, not conversational English (e.g., "Card" specifically means "Main Deck card").
- **Can't Beats Can**: Prohibitive effects take precedence over permissive ones.

## Deck Construction (Verified)
- **Champion Legend**: 1 per deck. Dictates **Domain Identity** (Fury [R], Calm [G], Mind [B], Body [O], Chaos [P], Order [Y]).
- **Chosen Champion**: 1 starting in the Champion Zone. Must match the Legend's tag.
- **Main Deck**: Exactly 40 cards. Limit 3 per named card (including Chosen Champ).
- **Signature Cards**: Limit 3 total per deck; must match Legend's tag.
- **Rune Deck**: Exactly 12 Runes. Must match Domain Identity.
- **Battlefields**: Exactly 3 unique battlefields (shuffled/randomized based on Mode).

## Turn Structure (Verified ABCD Protocol)
1. **Awaken Phase**: Ready all Game Objects (untap).
2. **Beginning Phase**: 
   - **Step 1: Scoring**: Score +1 Point for each Held battlefield.
   - **Step 2: Logic**: If Legend/Champ is missing, controller draws 1 card.
3. **Channel Phase**: Take 2 Runes from the Rune Deck.
4. **Draw Phase**: Draw 1 card from the Main Deck. Rune Pools empty after this.
5. **Action Phase**: No structure. Discretionary actions (Move, Play, Activate) occur here.
6. **End of Turn Phase**: 
   - **Step 1: Cleanup**: Stun wears off. Units heal.
   - **Step 2: Expiration**: "This turn" effects end. Rune Pools empty.

## Combat Mechanics
- **Standard Move**: Exhaust unit to move Base ↔ Battlefield.
- **Showdown**: A window where players alternate Actions/Reactions.
- **Damage**: **Showdown damage is persistent** until end of Turn or Combat Cleanup.
- **Lethal Damage**: Non-zero damage ≥ Might [M].
- **Tie**: If both sides have units left or both are wiped, it's a tie. Attackers are recalled.

## Key Logic & Terminology
- **Might [M]**: Attack/Defense stat. If < 0, treated as 0 for damage dealing.
- **Energy**: Generated by **Exhausting** a Rune.
- **Power**: Generated by **Recycling** a Rune (to bottom of Rune Deck).
- **[A]**: Universal power (any domain).
- **[C]**: Power of this card's specific domain.
- **Burn Out**: Drawing from empty deck. Opponent gets +1 Point; recycle trash into deck.
- **Mighty**: A status (not keyword) for units with 5+ Might.
