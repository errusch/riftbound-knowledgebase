## Core Terms & Symbology
- **[M] (Might)**: The attack/power stat of a unit.
- **[A] (Action/Cost)**: Energy/Rune cost to hide or play cards.
- **[C] (Connection/Equip Cost)**: Specific cost for attaching Equipment.
- **Recycle**: Move cards to the bottom of the deck. Note: Players always recycle their own cards regardless of who triggers it.
- **Banish**: Remove from the game.
- **Showdown**: The state where units interact (Combat or Non-combat).
- **Mighty**: A unit with 5 or more Might. This is a state, not a keyword.

## Scoring Architecture
- **Conquer (+1)**: Take control of a battlefield you didn't control and haven't scored yet this turn.
- **Hold (+1)**: Control a battlefield at the start of your turn.
- **The Final Point (Rule 632.1)**: To win via conquering, you must have already scored all other battlefields that turn. Otherwise, draw 1 card instead of scoring.
- **Burn Out**: Attempting to draw from an empty deck. Recycle trash, shuffle, and choose an opponent to score 1 point. If no trash exists, the opponent wins immediately.

## Movement & Tactical Zones
- **Standard Move**: Main Phase only. Exhaust unit to move Base ↔ Battlefield.
- **Ganking**: Move Battlefield ↔ Battlefield using the standard move.
- **Group Move**: Move multiple units to the same destination simultaneously by exhausting all.
- **Showdown Injection**: Units can only enter a showdown via [Action] or [Reaction] effects.

## Spell Speeds & Types
- **[Action]**: Slow speed. Playable on your turn or during Showdowns.
- **[Reaction]**: Fast speed. Playable any time (LIFO).
- **[Hidden]**: Pay [A] to place face-down at a battlefield you control. Can react for [0] on subsequent turns. Units/Targets are restricted to that battlefield.

## Resource System
- **Energy**: Generated by exhausting a Rune. Used for numerical costs.
- **Power**: Generated by recycling a Rune. Used for domain (color) costs.
- **Deterministic Ramping**: 12-card Rune deck ensures no "Mana Screw."
